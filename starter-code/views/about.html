About the founder

Since he was little Ignacio has always been an animal lover. Always striving to be the best in his expertise, Ignacio has studied the behaviour of almost every animal alive in the planet. He has travelled to over more than 60 countries and his research for animal preservation still continues to be his main goal. In 2000, he decided to open an animal conservation zoo and since then he has managed over 2000 species.
